Cameroon

A water distribution project in the village of Mungoa-goa. Mungoa-goa is located on a hillside in the northwest province of Cameroon and consists of approximately 1000 people. The upper third of the people of Mungoa-goa must walk at least 10 min downhill (~300 ft vertical elevation change) for access to clean water. EWB-USA GT has partnered with the people of Mungoa-goa to provide a solar powered well and distribution system to supply an adequate amount of water according to World Health Organization standards for 20 years.

La Lima

The people of Los Angeles do not have indoor water faucets & sinks. The existing system distributes water outside of the house into sinks or buckets which the people use for drinking, cooking and cleaning. The water only runs for two four hour periods each day.
