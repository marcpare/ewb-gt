EWB-USA supports community-driven development programs worldwide by collaborating with local partners to design and implement sustainable engineering projects, while creating transformative experiences and responsible leaders.

About the GT Chapter

Want to apply your engineering classwork to something that makes a difference? EWB at Georgia Tech is student-run and organizes trips to Africa, South America, and more. Lately we've been designing wells and making maps for places that no one has ever mapped before.

Projects

With a focus on making clean water accessible, EWB plans, designs, and executes projects around the world. Students are heavily involved with every step in the process.
